# Sky-Hub

Welcome to Sky-Hub, a collection of scripts and tools primarily for Roblox games, developed by yofriendfromschool1. This repository aims to provide enhanced gameplay experiences and utilities for a wide range of popular Roblox titles.

* **Superfling Script:** A powerful script that offers advanced "fling" mechanics with customizable options for velocity and radius, designed to enhance your in-game interactions.
    * **Noclip & Fly:** Automatically enabled for enhanced movement. Fly is mobile supported.
    * **Fling Velocity:** Adjustable to control the force of flinging.
    * **Fling Radius Types:**
        * **Spread Radius:** Reduces effectiveness but widens the fling radius.
        * **Focus One Radius:** Increases effectiveness but narrows the fling radius.

* **Trolling GUIs:** Front-end (FE) trolling GUIs are available for various games to add an element of fun and chaos to your gameplay.

* **Script Management:** Sky-Hub includes various other scripts and tools, often categorized for specific purposes or games.

## How to Use

1.  **Download:** Obtain the desired script or the main Sky-Hub package from this repository.
2.  **Execute:** Use a compatible Roblox executor (e.g., an exploit or a custom game client) to inject and run the scripts within your Roblox game.
3.  **Configure:** Follow any specific instructions or configuration files (like `SkyHub.txt` or `lynx.txt`) to set up the scripts for your preferred games and features.

**Note:** The use of third-party scripts and executors in Roblox may violate the Roblox Terms of Service and could lead to account penalties. Use with caution and at your own risk.
